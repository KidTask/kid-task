User Story

As a casual user on Kidtasktic, Mom wants to add a new task for Tim.

Use Case/Interaction Flow

precondition: Mom wants Tim to clean room. She put the new chore on the app.

post condition: Tim's room is clean and mom gives Tim an extra hour before his bedtime.

  • Tim opens Kidtasktic, and sees the new chore "clean room"
  • Website loads the page with list of chores
  • Tim opens the chore
  • Website opens the "clean room" page and loads the steps
  • Tim does the chore with each step
  • Website stays same
  • Tim cleaned his room! He clicks the button "finished"
  • Website puts a check mark next to the "clean room" task
  • Mom sees that the chore is finished on her phone
  • Website loads chores list on her phone
