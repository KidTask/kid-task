Situation

Mamma Mia has had an extremely busy day. She comes home and it's already time for dinner. Her kid was bugging to turn on the tv. Mia declared, "If your room is clean, clothes are put away, and homework is doneâ€”yes, you may watch TV."

Mia goes back to making dinner as she hears the tv blaring in the next room. She steps into the next room as she passes her kids bedroom. Her eye catches the clothes that were supposed to be put away, still on the bed.

If only there was an easier way to keep track of the list of tasks she gives her child to ensure she is raising a responsible individual.

Use Case

Scenario

Mia logs in and adds her child as a user. She goes to add tasks and incentive value for the task. She saves and they are sent to the kids profile.

Precondition

Mia needs to be logged in to add child. Child profile need to be selected before adding tasks.

Postcondition

Mia has added tasks and incentives to her her child's profile.

Interaction Flow

Mia logs into her account

App returns parent dashboard

She clicks to add new child profile

App returns add child form

Mia inputs information and clicks save

App returns confirmation with page that contains child's profile icon

Mia clicks profile icon to add tasks

App returns kids profile page; dashboard to add tasks and incentives, view completed tasks, etc.

Mia clicks to add task

App returns task and incentive input section

Mia inputs task and assigns value to task; clicks to save

App returns child's profile dashboard as affordance.
