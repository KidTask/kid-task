Rem Persona

User Story

As a kid user, Rem wants to earn rewards by completing tasks

Demographics

Name: Rem Smith

Age: 4

Job: Clean room

Tech

Device: Amazon Fire 6 (Fire OS 7)

Internet: Beginner

Tablet Skills: Beginner

Goals

Rem wants to earn screen time and snacks by:

  • Picking up clothes
  • Picking up toys
  • Brushing teeth
  • Completing other tasks

Frustrations

  • Reading
  • Cleaning is boring
  • Not knowing where to start
  • Not knowing when he's finished

Situation

Rem got home after a long half-day of preschool. Another child played with
his favorite toy and Rem is feeling frustrated. All he wants to do is watch
Sesame Street and drink his favorite Capri Sun. But his dad said he can't
watch tv until his room is clean.
