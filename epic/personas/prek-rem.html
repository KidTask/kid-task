Rem Persona

User Story

As a kid user, Rem wants to earn rewards by completing tasks

Demographics

Name: Nya Lindell

Age: 32

Job: Small Business Owner - Cupcake Shop

Tech

Device: Surface Pro (Windows Pro 10)

Internet: Sufficient

Social: Above Average

Online Shopping: Excellent

Goals

Nya wants to improve her design skills to attract more customers. Specifically:

  • She wants to be able to design online and physical fliers for specials
  • She wants to be able to edit unique backgrounds into pictures
  • She wants to add custom pics to her website

Frustrations

  • Nya is struggling to figure out layers in Photoshop
  • She can't go back and fix changes to edited layers
  • She wants to overlay textures onto backgrounds
